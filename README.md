# Privacy Policy for MuslimApp

**Last Updated: June 04, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how MuslimApp ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your experience with our app is safe and secure.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**a. Location Data:** To provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction, the App requires access to your device's location. Your coordinates are processed locally on your device and are **not** stored on any external servers.

**b. Locally Stored Data:** The App uses a local database (Room) to store your personal progress, including Quran bookmarks, Tasbeeh counts, and settings. This data stays on your device.

**c. Voluntary Information:** When you report a correction or contact support, the information you provide via email is used only to improve the App.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Play Services for location and store functionality, and Al-Quran Cloud API for Quranic text. No personal identifiers are shared with these services.

## 3. Permissions

The App requests Location, Internet, and Notification permissions to function correctly.

## 4. Security

Your data is stored locally and is as secure as your device. We do not sell or trade your data.
